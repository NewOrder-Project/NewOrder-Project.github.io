In the new publication 'Who said what? Confusing scientists and laypeople on social media.' Eva Rudholzer and Sonja Utz investigate, whether people can tell if a science-related post comes from a scientist or a layperson. Their findings highlight the importance of visible sender cues for scientific posts. If you want to know more, have a look at the paper here.
